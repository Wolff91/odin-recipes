Worlds best lasagna!

The worlds best lasagna is an easy to follow, super delicious recipe that will leave you with a wonderful tasting experience

Ingredients:

  • 1kg of Minced meat
  • 1 big onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 chilipepper
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • 1 canned tomatoes, chopped
  • Fresh celery
  • 2 red peppers
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Mornay sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  1. Cut onion, garlic and chilipepper into fine pieces
  2. Put the chopped stuff into a big pot
  3. When the chopped stuff is soft add the minced meat to the pot
  4. While the meat is cooking, chop the celery, fresh tomatoes and red peppers
  5. When there is no more raw meat add the celery
  6. Let it cook for 10 minutes
  7. Add the fresh tomatoes and red peppers
  8. let them cook for 5 minutes
  9. Add the canned tomatoes, salt, pepper, oregano and basil
  10. Let it cook for 30 minutes under a lit at middle temperature
  11. Turn on the oven to 200 degrees celcius
  12. Put the sauce in the bottom then mornay sauce and finish with lasagna sheets
  13. Keep doing that until it's done and top it off with mornay sauce and grated cheese
  14. Let it bake for 30 minutes in the oven
